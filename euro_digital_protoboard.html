EURORACK DIGITAL PROTOBOARD

I was tired of using protoboard for big projects. It takes too much time to get something working. Therefore I designed this PCB as a multi-purpose digital eurorack pcb.



it houses a STM32f030K6 (TQFP32) easily solderable and 0805 SMD components. With the help of jumper several connections can be made.



It is now easier to build stuff, thanks China and Elecrow !!
