SYNTHI BISCUIT 2

A try at a smaller version of the synthi biscuit. This time using a cheaper ARM based microcontroller (STM32f0) and using PWM as DAC. The PCB also had a footprint for a MAX5713 DAC but it seems that the part is now obsolete. Shame.



The boards are in and being populated !
