NOISE SYNTH

A particular noise synth that uses external signal (microphone or line input) as sound mangler. Small fragment of incoming sounds are recorded and then playback with different variable degree of noise, bit crush, sampling rate modification. Considered the very little amount of RAM available in the current state of the project (a tiny ARM stm32f0) the recording buffer is very small, hence the signal will only serve as a kind of "vco".

It also has two VCO, one filter, a mixer and all the usual thing.

not build yet but maybe a proper DSP will be more enjoyable and bring the idea to a more higher level in sonic mayhem.
