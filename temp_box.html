TEMP BOX AND OTHER MISC. STUFF

I wanted to make use of the remaining OLED screen and 7 segment display. With that, I wanted to use the PIC24 architecture. Considering the massive shortage on chip (can't find stm32 anymore, at a reasonable price) I decided to use Microchip's PIC24.
