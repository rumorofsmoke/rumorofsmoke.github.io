UPDATE on the video circuit: Bypass

Thinking about bringing this lofi video processor into the realm of modular synth.

I have a NAND gate of CD4011 left and I thought it'd be cool to have the bypass control linked to the micro. A soft push button is always sexier than a bulky toogle switch.

I used the left NAND gate as a gate for the blanking signal.
